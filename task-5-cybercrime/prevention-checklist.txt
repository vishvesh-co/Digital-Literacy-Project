Stay Safe Online: Prevention Checklist
The following checklist is tailored for college students in India and focuses on practical, actionable steps.

•	Do not share personal information (phone number, address, college ID) with strangers online.
•	Set all social media accounts to private and review your follower/friend list regularly.
•	Screenshot and document any harassing messages before blocking or reporting the account.
•	Report cyberbullying directly on the platform using the built-in report/block features.
•	Never respond to provocative or harassing messages — engaging often escalates the situation.
•	Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on all accounts to prevent impersonation.
•	UPI Safety: Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone, even if they claim to be from your bank or a government agency.
•	UPI Safety: Be alert to 'collect payment' requests — accepting such a request sends money out of your account, not into it.
•	Talk to a trusted adult, college counsellor, or peer support group if you or someone you know is being targeted online.
•	Report cybercrime at the National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in or call Helpline 1930 (available 24x7).
