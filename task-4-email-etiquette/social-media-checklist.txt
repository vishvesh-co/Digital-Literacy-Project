Social Media Do's and Don'ts:

Do's:
•	Keep your profiles professional – use a clear, appropriate display name and photo.
•	Think before you post – consider how your content would appear to future employers or professors.
•	Use privacy settings to control who sees your personal information.
•	Engage constructively and respectfully in all online discussions.
•	Give credit when sharing others' work, ideas, or images.

Don'ts:
•	Do not share personal details such as your phone number, home address, or college ID publicly.
•	Do not post offensive, discriminatory, or politically inflammatory content.
•	Do not engage in or tolerate cyberbullying, harassment, or online mob behaviour.
•	Do not share unverified news, rumours, or misinformation – verify before sharing.
•	Do not use social media to vent frustrations about specific classmates, faculty, or institutions.
